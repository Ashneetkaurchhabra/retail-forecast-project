Why LSTM Outperforms Traditional Models

🧠
Learns Non-Linear Patterns
ARIMA and Holt-Winters assume linearity. Retail sales have complex non-linear relationships between holidays, seasons, and promotions that LSTM captures naturally.
📆
Long-Range Memory
LSTM gates selectively remember patterns from weeks or months ago — like annual Black Friday spikes — which a simple moving window cannot replicate.
🎯
No Stationarity Assumption
ARIMA requires differencing to handle non-stationary data. LSTM works directly on raw scaled values without any preprocessing assumptions.
🔄
Adapts to Trend Shifts
Holt-Winters uses fixed smoothing parameters. LSTM dynamically adapts its internal weights to handle structural breaks and sudden demand shifts.
📉
Captures Volatility
Retail sales exhibit high weekly volatility. LSTM's recurrent connections model the variance in sequences more accurately than parametric models.
🚀
Scales with Data
With 45 stores and 3 years of weekly data, LSTM has enough samples to learn generalizable patterns that improve with more training data.
